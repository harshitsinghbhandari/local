# Idea 14 — AI SDR for B2B Sellers

**Category:** Small Business Services
**One-liner:** Replace your $80k SDR with an agent that runs your outbound, books meetings, and never sleeps.

## The business
- Target: B2B founders + sales teams under 10 reps. Currently using Apollo + manual workflows.
- Pricing: $499/mo for 1,000 contacted prospects, $1,499/mo for 5,000, $4,999/mo unlimited + multichannel.
- Revenue path: 20 customers × $1k avg = $20k MRR.

## What the AI agents do
- ICP definition through onboarding conversation.
- Build target lists via Apollo/Clay/Sales Nav API.
- Research each prospect: recent funding, role change, tech stack, posts, mutual connections.
- Generate hyper-personalized first emails + 4-step sequences.
- Multi-channel: email, LinkedIn, even cold call follow-ups (Gemini Live).
- Handle replies: classify intent, book meetings via Calendar API, hand-off to human for qualified leads.

## Why it can be AI-run
- SDRs spend 60% of their time on research + email drafting. Both are model-native tasks.
- Reply handling = classification + scheduling = pure agent territory.

## 90-day go-to-market
- Wk 1–2: Build for one vertical (B2B SaaS founders selling to other founders).
- Wk 3–6: Cold outreach to other founders (use the product on itself = best demo).
- Wk 7–12: Case studies → ProductHunt / LinkedIn launch.

## Tech stack
- Gemini for prospect research synthesis + email generation.
- Vertex AI embeddings for ICP fit scoring.
- Apollo/Clay/Hunter APIs for data.
- Gmail + LinkedIn API for sending.

## Risks & moats
- **Risk:** Hyper-competitive space (Clay, Apollo, 11x.ai). Win on price + ease + a single sharp vertical.
- **Moat:** Your customer's reply data trains better sequences over time.
