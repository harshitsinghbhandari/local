# Idea 11 — AI Financial Coach for the Underbanked

**Category:** Money & Financial Access
**One-liner:** A personal CFO for the 25% of Americans who can't afford one and don't trust their bank.

## The business
- Target: Underbanked / credit-thin households, immigrant workers, gig workers. Massive in US, India (Bharat), LatAm, SE Asia.
- Pricing: $5/mo direct, OR free with affiliate revenue from products recommended (credit-builder cards, high-yield savings).
- Revenue path: 2000 users × $5 = $10k MRR + affiliate revenue.

## What the AI agents do
- Multi-language onboarding (Spanish, Hindi, Tagalog, Vietnamese).
- Budget plan generation given income, rent, family size.
- Credit-score growth roadmap with specific actions.
- Bill negotiation: call your cable/internet provider, negotiate lower rate.
- Subscription audit: cancel forgotten subs via merchant APIs.
- Emergency fund coaching with progress tracking.
- Tax prep light (W-2 only) for EITC-eligible filers.

## Why it can be AI-run
- A human financial advisor costs $200/hr — completely inaccessible to this segment.
- The advice itself is largely scripted. The unmet need is patience + accessibility + language.

## 90-day go-to-market
- Wk 1–2: Spanish + English MVP. WhatsApp + SMS first (no app friction).
- Wk 3–6: Partner with one local credit union or community org for outbound.
- Wk 7–12: Expand to Hindi for US-Indian + India domestic market.

## Tech stack
- Gemini for multilingual conversation + cultural-context advice.
- Gemini Live + Twilio for bill-negotiation phone calls.
- Plaid for account connection (US) + Account Aggregator framework (India).
- WhatsApp Business API as primary surface.

## Risks & moats
- **Risk:** Low ARPU segment — need volume. Affiliate revenue is the unlock.
- **Moat:** Trust in this segment is the moat. Once won, sticky for years.
