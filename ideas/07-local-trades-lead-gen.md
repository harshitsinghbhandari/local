# Idea 07 — AI Lead Gen for Local Trades

**Category:** Small Business Services
**One-liner:** Plumbers, electricians, roofers — fully booked next month without lifting a finger.

## The business
- Target: Owner-operator trades businesses ($200k–$2M revenue) who hate marketing and currently rely on word-of-mouth or Yelp.
- Pricing: $299/mo + $20 per qualified lead, OR $799/mo all-inclusive.
- Revenue path: 30 contractors × $500 avg = $15k MRR within 90 days.

## What the AI agents do
- Set up Google Business Profile, optimize listing weekly.
- Auto-generate location-specific landing pages ("emergency plumber in [neighborhood]").
- Run + optimize Google Local Service Ads with budget pacing.
- Inbound call answering (Gemini Live) → qualify → schedule → CRM entry.
- Review request automation post-job (text customers 2 days after).
- Quote generation from job photos sent over MMS.

## Why it can be AI-run
- Trades businesses don't need humans answering calls at 11pm — they need consistent capture.
- All the work (ads, SEO, qualifying, scheduling, follow-up) is repetitive, rule-driven.

## 90-day go-to-market
- Wk 1–2: Build for one trade (HVAC — high ticket size, urgent demand) in one metro.
- Wk 3–6: Cold call/door-knock 200 contractors. Offer first month free.
- Wk 7–12: Replicate to plumbing + electrical. Recruit a metro-by-metro affiliate program.

## Tech stack
- Gemini Live for inbound phone (Twilio integration).
- Vertex AI for ad copy + landing page generation.
- Google Ads API + Google Business Profile API.
- BigQuery for lead attribution dashboard.

## Risks & moats
- **Risk:** Trades are slow tech adopters. Heavy hand-holding for first 10 customers.
- **Moat:** Their CRM data + ad accounts live in your system. Switching = restart from zero.
