# Idea 16 — AI Content & SEO Agency

**Category:** Small Business Services
**One-liner:** Replace your $5k/month content agency. We rank you, you sleep.

## The business
- Target: SMBs and SaaS startups doing inbound marketing. Currently overpaying agencies or hiring underwhelming freelancers.
- Pricing: $499/mo (4 articles + SEO), $1,499/mo (12 articles + technical SEO + reporting), $4,999/mo full agency replacement.
- Revenue path: 20 customers × $1,200 avg = $24k MRR.

## What the AI agents do
- Keyword research (Ahrefs/SEMrush API + custom intent modeling).
- Content brief generation with target keywords, SERP analysis, outline.
- Write the article. Original research where possible (web scraping + synthesis).
- Internal linking strategy.
- Publish via WordPress/Webflow/Ghost API.
- Technical SEO audits — flag broken links, slow pages, missing schema.
- Monthly performance reports with action items.

## Why it can be AI-run
- This is exactly what AI writes well + measures quantitatively (rankings, traffic).
- Agencies charge for time their juniors spend on what models do in seconds.

## 90-day go-to-market
- Wk 1–2: Build end-to-end pipeline for one CMS (WordPress).
- Wk 3–6: Outreach to small SaaS founders + DTC brands. Free first article as proof.
- Wk 7–12: Show ranking screenshots in marketing. Compound on word-of-mouth.

## Tech stack
- Gemini long-context for SERP synthesis + outline + draft.
- Vertex AI Search for grounding citations.
- WordPress REST API, Ghost API, Webflow API.
- Google Search Console + Ahrefs APIs.

## Risks & moats
- **Risk:** AI content discount by Google — quality bar must be high. Differentiate with original research / data analysis pieces.
- **Moat:** Per-customer brand voice + topic cluster ownership compounds.
