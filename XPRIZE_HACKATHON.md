## The Challenge

> "Build a real business that makes real impact."

Create a functioning business within 90 days that:
- Is **operated by AI agents** (doing work normally done by large engineering/ops teams)
- Has **real customers** and generates **real revenue**
- Fits into one of 5 categories below

---

## Five Categories

1. **Education & Human Potential**
2. **Entrepreneurship & Job Creation**
3. **Small Business Services**
4. **Money & Financial Access**
5. **Professional Services Access**

---

## Prize Structure

| Prize | Amount | # of winners |
|---|---|---|
| 1st Place | $500,000 | 1 |
| 2nd Place | $200,000 | 1 |
| 3rd – 5th Place | $100,000 | 3 |
| Runner-up | $50,000 | 15 |
| Category Winner (1 per category) | $50,000 | 5 |

**Total: $2,000,000**

⚠️ A project is only eligible for a maximum of **one** prize.

---

## Technical Requirements

- Must use **at least one Google Cloud product**.
- Any LLM functionality must use the **Gemini API for at least one LLM call**.
- Project must be **newly created** during the hackathon period (May 19 – Aug 17, 2026).
- Must demonstrate how AI transforms business workflows.

---

## Submission Requirements

You'll need to provide all of the following:

### 1. Code Repository
- Public or private repo
- Shared with: `testing@devpost.com` and `judging@hacker.fund`

### 2. Video Demo
- **< 3 minutes**
- Shows the functioning project on its intended device
- Hosted on YouTube, Vimeo, or Youku
- Must show AI live in production
- No unauthorized third-party trademarks/copyrighted material

### 3. Written Narrative (500–1000 words)
- Team workflow
- Human vs. AI responsibilities
- Economic impact

### 4. Financial Documentation
- Total revenue from third-party customers (USD)
- Monthly revenue breakdown (May–August 2026)
- Stripe exports / bank statements / P&L statements
- Total costs with one-sentence descriptions
- Marketing & customer acquisition spending
- Related-party revenue disclosed **separately**

### 5. User / Customer Evidence
- Number of individual users
- User demographic info
- Customer testimonials & contact info

### 6. Production Evidence
- Agent execution logs
- API usage records
- Dashboard screenshots
- Corporate ID (if submitting as org)

### 7. Text Description
- How the project meets requirements and category fit

---

## Judging

### Stage One — Pass/Fail
Baseline viability + theme fit.

### Stage Two — Equally Weighted Across Three Criteria
1. **Business Viability** — "Teams must launch a real business during the hackathon, acquire real users, and generate real revenue."
2. **AI-Native Operations** — Extent to which AI is live in production and executes key decisions.
3. **Category Impact** — Meaningful advancement within chosen category at scale.

## Strategic Notes (my take)

- This isn't a "build a cool demo" hackathon — **revenue and real users are hard requirements**. Plan for customer acquisition from day 1.
- Heavy documentation burden (financials, logs, testimonials) — set up tracking infrastructure early.
- 5 category prizes + 15 runner-ups = **25 total winning slots** on top of the top 5. Solid odds if you can clear the pass/fail bar.
- Google Cloud + Gemini API are mandatory — start there for stack decisions.
- 90-day clock from May 19 → Aug 17. The window has already started as of the compile date.
